Lord of the rings was published on the 29th of July, 1954 by J.R.R.Tolkien. It took 12 years for the trilogy to be written and is now known as one of the best book trilogies of all time. Lord of the rings has a word count of approximately 480,000 words and has roughly 750 - 800 characters.


Lord of the rings was later made into a trilogy of films:

  • The fellowship of the ring, released on the 10th of December 2001
  • The two towers, released on the 11th of December 2002
  • Return of the king, released on the 17th of December 2003
